The Money Is In The Database You Already Have

Every past client, old lead, and open-house signup on your list is a future deal. If they hear from you every week.

Most agents know this. Almost none act on it. The average database gets a holiday card, a market blast when things are slow, and silence the rest of the year. Then a past client lists with someone else, and the agent finds out on social media.

Nurture is not a campaign you run once. It is a weekly touch that gives your people a reason to reply. That is exactly what a Deal of the Week email does: one curated deal, written like a note from a friend who knows the market, sent every single week.

And it compounds. Do it for a month and you get opens. Do it for a year and your database learns to expect you every Tuesday. That consistency turns a contact list into a lifetime nurture asset, and it produces the listing appointments you never had to chase.

So Why Doesn't Every Agent Send One?
