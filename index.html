Doing This Yourself Every Week Is A Job

Sending one good Deal of the Week email takes more than people think.

First you have to find a listing worth sending, not just the newest one in the MLS feed. Then you verify the details so you're not sending a buyer bad numbers. Then you write it so it sounds like you, not like a bored MLS blast and not like AI wrote it. Then you do it again next week. And the week after that.

Call it 3 to 5 hours a week once you count sourcing, drafting, and rewriting the subject line until it stops sounding like everyone else's. The email itself isn't the hard part. Doing it every single week without missing one is the part that dies first.
