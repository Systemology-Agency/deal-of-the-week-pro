At signup: your name, email address, market city, zip codes, and how you sign your emails. Payment details are collected and stored by Stripe, our payment processor. We never see your full card number.

Your market information is used for one purpose: generating your weekly emails. Your email address receives your deliveries, your receipt from Stripe, and service messages about your subscription.

We do not sell your information, share it with third parties for marketing, or add you to unrelated email lists.

If you cancel, we keep your profile for 90 days in case you return, then delete it. You can request earlier deletion anytime.
